Boat - VR Game Design

In the VR game design of IAT 445 Immersive Environments, Our project for a horror puzzle / escape room type game offers a mini-horror experience that doesn't rely on excessive gore and traditional jump scares to create fear. Instead, we focus on building tension and suspense through atmospheric storytelling and a gradually unfolding narrative that culminates in a surprising ending.
