Walter Xu

(Known As Qiaohui Xu)

As a multidisciplinary designer with a strong foundation in visual, UX, and 3D design, I specialize in crafting immersive and emotionally resonant experiences. My passion for aesthetics transcends conventional boundaries and fuels my interest in fashion, visual storytelling, and interactive media. I have experience in game design, spatial composition, and digital prototyping, and I’m particularly drawn to projects that integrate narrative, space, and interaction. Whether designing for VR, games, or screen-based interfaces, I strive to maintain a high standard of visual elegance while solving real problems. My work reflects a thoughtful process of iteration, research, and conceptual depth.

Community Content Provider

In my academic pursuits, I am also actively contributing to a thriving gaming community on Steam. My commitment to this platform is more than just an entertainment activity; I am driven by a desire to improve the gaming experience and promote better interaction between players. By providing creative and unique solutions to various gaming problems, I seek to enhance the potential playability of games. My involvement in the Steam community is more than a hobby - it's a platform where I can make a tangible impact by improving the gaming experience for thousands of gamers around the world.
