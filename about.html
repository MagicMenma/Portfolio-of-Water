Walter Xu

(Known As Qiaohui Xu)

As a Visual Designer with an open-minded view of beauty, I am not bound by gender. I have a passion for beautifully designed clothing for everyone, and this obsession with aesthetics has driven me to maintain a high standard of elegance in my studies as a visual designer. I have diverse experience in design, including game, visual, industrial, and spatial design. I strive to create visually compelling and emotionally engaging designs that connect with people. This drive is reflected in my visual and textual ethos, which showcases my ability to think through a problem and iterate on a project over time to create innovative and impactful designs.

Community Content Provider

In my academic pursuits, I am also actively contributing to a thriving gaming community on Steam. My commitment to this platform is more than just an entertainment activity; I am driven by a desire to improve the gaming experience and promote better interaction between players. By providing creative and unique solutions to various gaming problems, I seek to enhance the potential playability of games. My involvement in the Steam community is more than a hobby - it's a platform where I can make a tangible impact by improving the gaming experience for thousands of gamers around the world.
