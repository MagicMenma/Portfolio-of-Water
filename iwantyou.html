I Want You

By weaving together the lyrical essence of the song 'I Want You,' I crafted a mesmerizing motion graphic animation in After Effect that brims with profound emotional depth. The seamless and visually striking transitions swiftly interlink the rich narrative within the video, creating a captivating spectacle.
Thanks to its satisfying performance, this video not only earned the accolade of 'Best Animation of the Semester' but also stood out as a highlight during the end-of-term project presentations, treating incoming IAT 100 students to a visual extravaganza.

Instructor: Susan Clements-Vivian

Creator: Walter Xu
